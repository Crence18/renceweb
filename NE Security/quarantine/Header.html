    WELCOME TO
    NE SECURITY LIMITED

    NE Security Ltd. are a north East Based, private and family-owned
    business, providing quality and reliable integrated security, including key
    holding & alarm response services, high-class manned guarding and
    mobile patrols to the commercial, distributionaland construction
    industries.


    We offer bespoke and high-quality security solutions to all our clients, and
    their safety and security is our upmost priority.

    ABOUT US

    We are a client driven, industry-leading team and we pride ourselves
    on delivering quality security solutions.

    Over the past 25 years we have developed a work ethic that puts our clients
    and customers at the heart of what we do. We're committed to keeping you
    safe, and we understand the level trust that you put into us.

    QUALITY AND ASSURANCE

    At NE Security Ltd., we pride ourselves in providing a service that meets, and often exceeds, the requirements and expectations of our clients. They can be
    assured that the services we provide are of the highest quality, and the safety and security of their company is of the upmost priority. We are popular
    with local businesses across the North East, and provide continuous professional development to each of our employees. This makes for a good relationship
    between the security officer and the organisation, or the individual, for whom we are providing security.

    In February 2004 we gained our IS0 9001:2008, and our system is audited manually. A great deal of time and effort has been invested into the quality
    standard to provide you with the very highest calibre of service a nd our fully trained operations managers are there to ensure everything runs smoothly.

    All sites are furnished with site specific risk assessment and assignment instructions to ensure we deliver security guards who are extremely dutiful.
